Bridging Research and Practice: Highlights from IEEE CG&A

IEEE CG&A Workshop
@ ACM SIGGRAPH Asia 2025, Hong Kong

Overview

This half-day workshop, hosted by IEEE Computer Graphics and Applications (CG&A), showcases the intersection of cutting-edge research and practical innovation in computer graphics. As part of CG&A's M1C2 (Magazine First, Conference Second) initiative, this event features recent CG&A publications selected for their relevance and impact.

Workshop Format

  • 12 invited 10-minute presentations by CG&A authors
  • Live poster session for in-depth discussion
  • Audience interaction and Q&A

Speakers

  • Invited plenary speaker (to be announced)
  • Authors of selected CG&A articles (to be announced)
